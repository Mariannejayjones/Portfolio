So.. Who am I?

What a great question and one that I’m still trying to answer but I hope this will suffice in the meantime.

The name’s Jones, Marianne Jones. I was born and raised in the UK (80’s kid) and then later moved to Portugal, so I’m fluent in both languages and a bit of a cultural mix.

More About Me

My university years were spent studying graphic design and even though I still love it to this day, I guess I never really found my calling professionally.
I spent most of my life looking for the right direction, like most of us I suppose, I took my time doing so.

Later in life, after various odd-jobs I was presented with an opportunity to be included in a full stack academy with a highly prestigious company in Porto and there it was - my lucky bug. I quickly found myself immersed in the world of frontend development and worked my way through courses to be one in the making.

At the end of the day, I´m creative by nature and a good spirit at heart and looking to take on any challenge or opportunity that may come my way.
